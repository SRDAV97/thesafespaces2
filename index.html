About Safe Spaces 2

Our mission to create secure, inclusive learning environments for students worldwide

Who We Are

Safe Spaces 2 is an international initiative dedicated to fostering inclusive educational environments where all students can thrive regardless of their background, identity, or circumstances.

Founded in 2020, we've grown to connect over 1,200 schools across 60+ countries, creating a global network of educators committed to student well-being and academic excellence.

1,200+ Schools
60+ Countries
5,000+ Educators

Our Mission

To create safe, inclusive learning environments that empower students to reach their full potential through global collaboration and innovative educational practices.

Our Vision

A world where every student has access to quality education in an environment that values diversity, inclusion, and mutual respect.

Our Values

Inclusion, Diversity, Equity, Collaboration, Innovation, and Respect form the foundation of everything we do at Safe Spaces 2.
